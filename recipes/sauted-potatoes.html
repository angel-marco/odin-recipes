Sauted Potatoes

Description

Indulge in the crispy goodness of skillet fried potatoes sliced to perfection. Learn how to make flavorful skillet home fried potatoes with this simple recipe

Ingredients

  • Potaoes
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Cut the potatoes in small pieces.
  2. Let them cook in boiling water
  3. Spread them on a skillet with oil until they turn to a brownish color
  4. Add any seasoning such as salt or onions.
