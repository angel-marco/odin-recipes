Pasta

Description

The simplest meal you can ever make. Sate your anger quickly with those homemade spagettis that can be made in minutes.

Ingredients

  • Spagettis
  • Tomato Sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil Water
  2. Add the spagettis
  3. Once the spagettis are cooked, remove the water and add the tomato sauce.
